Fork of the [suckless Surf browser](https://surf.suckless.org/) with extended functionality. Improved Wayland support and better site compatibility through custom WebKit patches.

**Waves** is a companion TUI addon with bookmark management (fuzzy search), project session support, and scriptable Lua keybindings.

No electron, no chromium, no 200MB baseline — just WebKit and a WM-friendly window.
