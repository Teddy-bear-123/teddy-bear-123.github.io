Fork of the [suckless Surf browser](https://surf.suckless.org/) with extended functionality. Improved Wayland support and better site compatibility through custom WebKit patches.

**Waves** is a companion TUI addon with:
- Bookmark management with fuzzy search
- Project session support (restore tab groups)
- Scriptable keybindings via Lua

The goal was a browser that gets out of your way — no electron, no chromium, no 200MB baseline. Just WebKit, a WM-friendly window, and a shell script for the rest.
